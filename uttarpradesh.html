Uttar Pradesh , lit. 'Northern Province'is a state in northern India. With over 200 million inhabitants, it is the most populous state in India as well as the most populous country subdivision in the world.[12] It was created on 1 April 1937 as the United Provinces of Agra and Oudh during British rule, and was renamed Uttar Pradesh in 1950, giving it the acronym UP.

The state is divided into 18 divisions and 75 districts, with the state capital being Lucknow, and Allahabad serving as the judicial capital. On 9 November 2000, a new state, Uttaranchal (now Uttarakhand), was carved from the state's Himalayan hill region.
The two major rivers of the state, the Ganges and Yamuna, join at Triveni Sangam in Allahabad and flow further east as Ganges. Other prominent rivers are Gomti and Saryu. The forest cover in the state is 6.1% of the state's geographical area. The cultivable area is 82% of total geographical area and net area sown is 68.5% of cultivable area.


1.Taj Mahal:

One of the seven wonders of the world, Taj Mahal is located on the banks of River Yamuna in Agra. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as a memorial for his third wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself.

Best Time to Visit :Year-round destination

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station :Yamuna Bridge Agra station

Nearest Bus stop :Taj View Chauraha bus stand

Nearest Airport :Agra Airport

2.Varanasi:

World's oldest living city, Varanasi - also known as Kashi (City of Life) and Benaras, is the spiritual capital of India. It is one of Hinduism's seven holy cities. The old city of Varanasi lies along the western banks of the Ganges.

Best Time to Visit :Year-round destination

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station :Varanasi Junction railway station

Nearest Bus stop :Varanasi Bus stop

Nearest Airport :Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport

3.Fatehpur Sikri:

Fatehpur Sikri is a town in the Agra district and a famous tourist attraction. A city predominantly made of red sandstone, Fatehpur Sikri was founded in 1571 century by Mughal Emperor Akbar. It is essentially a fortified city.

Best Time to Visit :Year-round destination

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station :Fatehpur Sikri station

Nearest Bus stop :Fatehpur Sikri bus stop

Nearest Airport :Kheria Airport

4.Prem Mandir,Vrindavan:

Imbued with elegance and grandeur, the Prem Mandir is a massive temple that was shaped by Jagadguru Shri Kripaluji Maharaj in 2001. Known as "Temple of God's love", this grand religious place is dedicated to Radha Krishna as well as Sita Ram.

Best Time to Visit :Year-round destination

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station :Vrindaban Road station

Nearest Bus stop :Pagal Baba Mandir bus station

Nearest Airport :Kheria Airport

5.Ayodhya:

Ayodhya, located on the banks of river Sarayu in Uttar Pradesh, is one of the seven sacred cities for Hindus. Ayodhya plays a critical role in the Hindu epic of Ramayana as it is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama.

Best Time to Visit :Year-round destination

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station :Ayodhya Junction

Nearest Bus stop :Ayodhya By Pass

Nearest Airport :Sri Ram Chandra International Airport

6.Triveni Sangam :

One of the holiest places in Central India, Triveni Sangam is located about 7 kilometres away from Civil Lines in Allahabad (Prayagraj). It is the meeting point of three rivers - Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati (which is a mythical river).

Best Time to Visit :October-June

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station :Daraganj station

Nearest Bus stop :Allahabad Busstand

Nearest Airport :Prayagraj Airport
