Explore Maharastra:



Maharashtra is a state in the western peninsular region of India occupying a substantial portion of the Deccan Plateau. Maharashtra is the second-most populous state in India as well as the third-most populous country subdivision in the world. It was formed on 1 May 1960 by splitting the bilingual Bombay State, which had existed since 1956, into majority Marathi-speaking Maharashtra and Gujarati-speaking Gujarat.


The state is divided into 6 divisions and 36 districts, with the state capital being Mumbai, also the most populous urban area in India and Nagpur serving as the winter capital. The Godavari and the Krishna are the two major rivers in the state. Marathi is the most widely spoken language and is also the official language of the state.

1.Mumbai:

Mumbai is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. According to the United Nations, as of 2018, Mumbai is the second-most populous city in the country after Delhi and the seventh-most populous city in the world with a population of roughly 20 million.

Weather : Summer (February – July), Winter (october-february), Monsoon (july – September)

Nearest Bus stop : Cbd Belapur-Near Bridge never-ending

2.Ajantha caves:

The Buddhist Caves in Ajanta are approximately 30 rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments dating from the 2nd century BCE to about 480 CE in the Aurangabad district of Maharashtra state in India. The caves include paintings and rock-cut sculptures described as among the finest surviving examples Indian art.

Best Time to Visit : July (Beginning of Monsoon) to February (Early Summer)

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (December – February), Monsoon (June – September)

Nearest Railway Station : Pachara Junction railway station

Nearest Bus stop : Ajanta Ajanta Bus Stop

Nearest Airport : Aurangabad Airport

3.Mahabaleshwar:

Mahabaleshwar is a small town and a municipal council in Satara district in the Indian state of Maharashtra.It is a place of pilgrimage for Hindus because the Krishna river has its origins here. The British colonial rulers developed the town as a hill station.

Best Time to Visit : march-june

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – Mid March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station : Khed Railway Station

Nearest Bus stop : Mahabaleshwar Bus station

Nearest Airport : Pune International Airport

4.Ellora Caves:

Ellora is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in the Aurangabad district of Maharashtra, India. It is one of the largest rock-cut Hindu temple cave complexes in the world, featuring Hinduism in particular and few Buddhist and Jain monuments with Artwork dating from the 600–1000 CE period.

Best Time to Visit : July-September

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – Mid March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station : Lasur Railway Station

Nearest Bus stop : Adalat Bus Stop

Nearest Airport : Chhatrapathi Sambhaji Maharaj International Airport

5.Nashik

Nashik is an ancient city and the largest city in the northern region of the Indian state of Maharashtra. Situated on the banks of river Godavari, Nashik is the fourth largest city in Maharashtra, after Mumbai, Pune and Nagpur. Nashik is well known for being one of the Hindu pilgrimage sites of the Kumbh Mela.

Best Time to Visit : October to March (Winter)

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – Mid March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station : Nashik ROAD Train station

Nearest Bus stop : Dwaraka Circle bus stop

Nearest Airport : Chhatrapathi Sambhaji Maharaj International Airport

6.Aurangabad:

Aurangabad is a city in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is the administrative headquarters of Aurangabad district and is the largest city in the Marathwada region. Located on a hilly upland terrain in the Deccan Traps, Aurangabad is the fourth-most populous urban area in Maharashtra with a population of 1,175,116.

Best Time to Visit : October to March

Weather : Summer (April – June), Winter (October – Mid March), Monsoon (July – September)

Nearest Railway Station : Deoria Kurhma Naresh Halt station

Nearest Bus stop : Aurangabad bus stop

Nearest Airport : Chikalthana Airport
